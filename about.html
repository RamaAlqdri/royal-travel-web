About Royal Travel

Royal Travel

Our History

Royal Travel was founded in August 2016 in Spain to offer exclusive, high-end travel experiences in Indonesia for English speaking clients.

With a legally registered business and strong roots in both Spanish professionalism and Indonesian culture, we have built trusted partnerships with villa owners, yacht providers, private jet operators, and premium golf resorts over the years. Every trip is designed to offer comfort, beauty, and a personal touch.

Having welcomed numerous clients since our inception, our consultants speak fluent English and are passionate about sharing Indonesia’s most beautiful destinations in a professional and respectful way.

We continue to grow with a commitment to integrity, innovation, and service excellence, helping each guest experience Indonesia in a personal and unforgettable way.

Our Services

We curate extraordinary luxury experiences that transcend traditional travel. Our bespoke services are designed to transform your journey into an unparalleled adventure of comfort, exclusivity, and personalized indulgence.

  • Luxury Villas & Resorts

    Experience the pinnacle of accommodation with our meticulously selected luxury villas and five-star resorts. Each property offers unparalleled comfort, breathtaking views, and personalized service that redefines hospitality.

  • Private Yacht Charters

    Sail through Indonesia's most pristine waters on exclusive private yachts. From intimate vessels to grand maritime experiences, we offer bespoke sailing adventures that combine luxury, privacy, and unparalleled exploration.

  • Private Jet Travel

    Elevate your journey with our private aviation services. Experience the ultimate in comfort, speed, and discretion as you traverse Indonesia's stunning landscapes with first-class, personalized air travel.

  • Prestigious Golf Retreats

    Indulge in world-class golfing experiences at Indonesia's most exclusive courses. From tropical island greens to meticulously designed landscapes, we offer golf retreats that combine sport, luxury, and breathtaking scenery.
